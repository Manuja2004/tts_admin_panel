Help Center

How can we help you?

Browse the frequently asked questions below or contact our support team directly.

Referrals

Go to Referrals → Add Referral from the sidebar. Fill in the student's name, phone number, and the course they're interested in. Click Submit Referral to complete the process. The referral will appear in My Referrals with a status of NEW.

  • NEW – The referral has been submitted and is awaiting contact.
  • CONTACTED – Our counselor has reached out to the student.
  • INTERESTED – The student has shown interest in enrolling.
  • CONVERTED – The student has enrolled and your commission is being processed.
  • NOT INTERESTED – The student declined after follow-up.

You can edit a referral's basic info (name, phone, email, course, batch) as long as the status is not CONVERTED. You can only delete a referral when its status is NEW. Once a referral is converted, it is locked and no changes can be made.

Each student's phone number must be unique in the system to avoid duplicate referral records. If a phone number already exists, a duplicate alert is shown. Please verify the number and try with a unique contact.

Earnings

Your commission is calculated as: Commission = Course Fee × Commission %. For example, if the course fee is ₹20,000 and the commission rate is 10%, you will earn ₹2,000 per converted student. Commission percentages are set by the admin and may vary by course.

Earnings appear automatically in your Earnings page as soon as a referral's status is updated to CONVERTED. The payment status will initially be Pending until the admin processes the payment.

Yes! In the Earnings section, you can filter by date range and course. The earnings trend chart and course-wise earnings chart provide a graphical overview of your earnings performance over time.

Payments

Payments are processed by the TTS admin and sent directly to the bank account or UPI ID registered in your Account → Bank Details. Please ensure your bank details are accurate and up to date.

Processing means the admin has initiated the payment transfer but it has not yet fully settled. Once the transaction is confirmed, the status will change to Paid.

Payment timelines vary and are managed by the TTS admin. Typically, payments are processed within 7–15 business days after a referral is confirmed as converted. If you have a concern, please contact our support team.

Account & Profile

Go to Account from the user menu (top right). Click Manage Bank Details or click Edit next to any field like IFSC Code, UPI ID, etc. Make your changes and click Save Changes.

Navigate to Account from the top-right dropdown and click Manage Profile. You can update your Name, Email, Location, and Profile Photo from there.

On the login page, click Forgot Password? and enter your registered email address. You will receive a link to reset your password. If you face any issues, contact our support team directly.
